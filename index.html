OUR MISSION.

We empower current and future generations of humanity by providing career placement and training opportunities focusing on creating a more sustainable future for ourselves and our posterity.

Critical Education
Our climate is affected by the products we use everyday. A major player of climate change is our use of fossil fuels causing an increased concentration of greenhouse gases in our atmosphere. This is devastating for our planet.
ClimateCorps offers an array of courses and subsequent job placement opportunities surrounding all of our focus areas in greater detail.
Learn more about the ClimateCorps Educational Curriculum.

Zero Waste
About 69% of all the plastics we use are not recyclable. Many think that recycling or using incinerators to get rid of waste is better than dumping in landfills, but in many cases it does more harm than good.
ClimateCorps is partnered with NetZero, a zero waste coalition, focused on helping communities achive net zero waste efficency. We also partner with the Global Gleaning Coalition.

Clean Water
Much of our water on this planet is being polluted daily by acids, oils, and carcinogens; deadly to wildlife and our ecosystem, while lessening our access to clean drinking water.
We are a part of the Clean Water State Revolving Fund (CWSRF), which provides education and implementation of converting hazardous waste streams into clean water.

Renewable Energy
Along with being harmful to our atmosphere upon burning these fuels, mining these sources is harmful to our ecosystem. Fossil fuels are also limited in availability and will not be able to be accessed forever.
Here at ClimateCorps, we are finding safer, more viable ways to generate energy such as Solar, and we provide classes and certification in this growing field.

Community Organizing
In 2019, the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA, 2019) reported that over 35 million people were food insecure. The Centers for Disease Control reports that only 1 in 10 Americans eat enough fruits and vegetables.
The best way to generate lasting change is with community organizing and activism. ClimateCorps have been involved in several community and sustainable farming initiatives and organizations.
